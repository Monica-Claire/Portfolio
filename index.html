About Me

Hi! I'm Monica. I am a senior instructional designer passionate about creating inclusive learning materials. As someone who struggled with learning and school growing up, I am passionate about ensuring every learner can succeed. I am excited to learn web development to improve my computer-based learning experiences for my learners.
